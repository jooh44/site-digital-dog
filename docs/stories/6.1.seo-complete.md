## Story

**As a** search engine,  
**I want** complete SEO metadata and schema on all pages,  
**so that** the website is properly indexed and appears in search results.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Unique meta tags on all pages
2. Schema.org complete (Organization, Article, FAQ)
3. Sitemap.xml updated with all pages
4. Robots.txt finalized
5. Alt text on all images
6. SEO audit passed
